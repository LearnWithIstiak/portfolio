Professional Profile

Shovon Mitra is a dedicated civil engineering professional currently serving as Deputy Director (Technical) at the Bangladesh Rural Electrification Board (BREB). With a strong foundation from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET), he brings technical depth, leadership, and a commitment to community development.
